FAQ

Questions, answered.

The things operators ask before they engage. If your question isn't here, just email us — we'll answer honestly.

01What exactly is the Sightline Audit?

It's a diagnostic. We identify the single operational constraint most responsible for limiting your growth, quantify what it's costing you, and map exactly where AI creates real leverage in your business. You get a clear, written deliverable — not a tool pitch, not a generic framework.

02How much does it cost?

There are three ways to engage. Tier 1 (Sightline Report) is the fully automated diagnostic. Tier 2 (Executive Review) adds human-reviewed findings and a live 90-minute session. Tier 3 (Sightline Architecture) is a custom strategic engagement, priced after a discovery conversation. Email us for current pricing on the tier that fits.

03How long does it take?

The Tier 1 report is delivered in 24 hours. The Tier 2 executive review is delivered in 72 hours, including the live session. We don't need months to find your line.

04Who is this for?

Operators running 10–50 person businesses with real revenue, who make the decisions. It works whether you've never touched AI or you've got tools scattered everywhere with no strategy connecting them. It's not built for enterprises needing procurement and committee sign-off, or for solo operators with no team yet.

05What if AI isn't actually the answer for my business?

Then we tell you that — plainly. We're not here to sell you AI. If the real leverage is somewhere else, we name the gap honestly and point you toward who can help. Our job is clarity, not selling you tools you don't need.

06Do I have to buy anything or install tools?

No. The audit is a diagnostic and a plan — you own it completely. There's no software to install, no retainer required, and no obligation to implement anything a particular way. What you do with the plan is entirely up to you.

07How do I get started?

Email us at hello@sightlineai.tech and tell us a bit about your business. We'll take it from there and walk you through the right starting point.

Still have a question?
