The Constraint

J’s Big Deal Company is not constrained by market demand, product quality, or team capacity. Sales reps loop in SE on every deal regardless of complexity. SE is a bottleneck. Quotes are slow. Reps lack product knowledge to qualify independently.

Quote speed improves dramatically. SE capacity freed for larger deals. Reps build product knowledge through the discovery process itself.
